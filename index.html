Co-op Work Term Report

- Reflecting on My Experiences

Abstract/Introduction

During my work term at JK EXIM, a stainless steel export company, I contributed to the development and enhancement of company's website. I worked remotely in a small IT team, focusing on improving frontend and backend functionality and setting up features to support e-commerce. This experience allowed me to gain practical knowledge in website development and collaboration.

Information About the Employer

JK EXIM is a prominent exporter of stainless steel products, catering to markets such as the US, Canada, the UK, Australia, and the UAE. While their core focus is on exports and manufacturing, the company has recently ventured into digitalization to enhance its online presence, a registered brand in the US. JK Exim aims to make stainless steel products accessible through e-commerce platforms. The IT team focus on exports, was vital in laying the foundation for their digital transformation.

Goals

  • Professional & Ethical Behaviour: Personal Organization/Time Management
    I aimed to enhance my time management skills to maintain a balance between work, rest, and personal life while also improving the quality of work I deliver by using planning strategies.

    How will these tasks benefit my next work experience?
    Learning to balance tasks, rest, and personal activities helps ensure productivity and reduces burnout in future roles.
  • Literacy: Technological Literacy
    I wanted to strengthen my proficiency in Next.js, Node.js and PHP to contribute effectively to projects and improve my overall programming skills.

    How will these tasks benefit my next work experience?
    By focusing on Node.js and Next.js, I improved my ability to write efficient code, debug effectively, and build scalable server-side applications.
  • Critical & Creative Thinking: Inquiry & Analysis
    My goal was to ask more precise questions and fully understand tasks before starting them to reduce errors and work more efficiently.

    How will these tasks benefit my next work experience?
    Asking clear and concise questions improved my ability to execute tasks accurately and collaborate better in team environments.

Reflections:

I successfully organized my time by creating daily schedules, setting reminders, and maintaining a consistent work-rest routine, allowing me to stay productive. Additionally, I became more confident my ability to write cleaner and more efficient backend solutions, which earned positive feedback on my contributions. I also enhanced my task analysis skills by asking the right questions to reduce confusion, ensuring alignment with project deliverables and improving overall efficiency.

Although I improved my Node.js skills and became more confident in writing efficient backend solutions, I didn't reach the advanced level of expertise I had hoped for in PHP. This was partly because of time limitations and the steep learning curve of some advanced concepts. I had to work with Laravel libraries, which I had never used before. While I managed to get by and complete my tasks, I feel that with more time, I could have explored Laravel in greater depth and gained a stronger understanding of its features and best practices, rather than just learning enough to get the job done.

Job Description



Key Responsibilities:

• Website Development & Maintenance: Help design, develop, and maintain the company's website to ensure optimal performance, functionality, and user experience.
• Software Development: Participate in the development and enhancement of internal software systems, writing clean, efficient code to support business operations.
• Database Management: Assist in managing and optimizing databases, ensuring data integrity, security, and availability for internal stakeholders.
• Debugging & Testing: Identify and resolve software bugs. Contribute to testing processes to maintain the stability and performance of existing systems.
• Collaboration: Work alongside cross-functional teams to gather requirements, contribute to design discussions, and assist in software project planning.
• Documentation: Prepare and update technical documentation, user guides, and reports for internal and external use.
• Continuous Learning: Stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in software development, applying this knowledge to improve our systems.

Qualifications:

• Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
• Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java, C++, or similar.
• Familiarity with web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
• Basic knowledge of databases (SQL, NoSQL).
• Excellent problem-solving skills with great attention to detail.
• Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team environment.
• Strong verbal and written communication skills.
• Passion for learning and keeping up with new technologies and trends. Benefits: • Gain hands-on experience in a real-world software development environment. • Receive mentorship from seasoned professionals in the industry. • Contribute to significant projects in the stainless steel export sector. • Potential for full-time employment upon successful completion of the internship.

Conclusions

This work term gave me hands-on experience in website development and digitalization for a traditional business transitioning into the e-commerce space. It reinforced the importance of collaboration, problem-solving, and adapting to new challenges. If asked to describe my experience, I would want readers to note my contributions to JK Exim's online presence and my growth as a developer through this internship.
